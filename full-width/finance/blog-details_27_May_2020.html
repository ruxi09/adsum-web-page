What is a VAT Bridging Loan?

  • By J T
  • Posted On May 27th, 202

Bridging loans for VAT are short-term loans used to pay the VAT cost on commercial property purchases. As things stand many property and land acquisitions attract a VAT payment of 20%. The VAT must be paid on top of the purchase price of the property at the time of its purchase, which can significantly elevate the costs of the transaction. However, the VAT can be recovered from HMRC after a month or two so it is only a temporary cost but it needs to be financed nonetheless.
