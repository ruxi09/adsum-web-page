R&D Tax Credit

  1. Home
  2. R&D Tax Credit

We at Adsum specialise in facilitating R&D Tax Credit Loans – the newest addition to our range of products. A financial instrument that helps you raise finance by using your R&D Tax Credit payments as collateral – the loan gives you access to the funds in the same year as your R&D spending.

R&D tax credit is a cash payment by the government to encourage companies to engage in these activities. Such a R&D tax credit loan is a financial instrument that uses future tax credit payments as collateral for a debt facility.

How can your firm benefit?

  • Make a claim
  • Seek Tax Credit Loan
  • Repayment

We send you a preliminary offer, you sign and accept.

Based on your potential claim, you can seek a R&D credit loan from Adsum and maintain your liquidity while we process your reclaim. The monthly rate of interest on R&D Tax Credit Loans averages somewhere between 1-1.5%. Additionally, 4-5% (of loan amount) is charged as loan set-up fees.

The HRMC approves and repays the claim to Adsum

Notable factors that Adsum considers:

• 51.53% claims are approved by the HMRC

• 77% CLAIMS are under GBP 50,000. Most claims range between GBP 40,000 – GBP 60,000

• More than 20% of claims are from London.

• As of 2017-18, 48,635 R&D tax credit claims have been made, out of which 42,075 are part of the SME R&D scheme. £4.3bn of R&D tax relief support was claimed for 2017-18, out of a corresponding R&D expenditure of £31.3bn.

Time frame

  • • HMRC aims to pay most SME R&D payable tax credit claims or contact the firm regarding the claim within 28 days in 95% of cases.
