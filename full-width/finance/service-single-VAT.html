VAT bridging loans allow construction traders to raise short term finance to cover the VAT element due on the purchase/redevelopment of a commercial property. As per the HMRC, £5.82 billion net payments in VAT reclaims were made to the construction industry in FY18-19.

Are you eligible?

Adsum gives out VAT loans to clients based on certain requirements. One of the requirements is to have a minimum of £50,000 in VAT loan. £50,000 VAT meant that the project is valued at £250,000 assuming that VAT is at 20% of project value. Hence, only construction traders with £250,000 and above annual turnover are qualified to apply for VAT bridging loan.
