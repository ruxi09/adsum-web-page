Film, TV & Video Game Tax Credit

  1. Home
  2. R&D Tax Credit
  3. More
Additionally, Adsum also aims to provide Film, TV and Video Games Tax Credit (VGTR) Loans

Process

  • Make a claim
  • Seek Tax Credit Loan
  • Repayment

A claim can be made up to 2 years from the end of a company’s accounting period.

The monthly rate of interest on Film & TV & VGTR Credit Loans averages between 0.5 -2.5% (however, it varies from case to case)

Typically, a loan arrangement can be put in place (and the loan paid to you) within 1-3 weeks of the start of the process.

Notable factors that Adsum considers:

A total of £1.1bn was paid out in creative industries tax reliefs in 2018-19, a 22% increase on the previous year’s figure of £900m. According to the HMRC, the amount of film and high-end TV tax relief has increased by around 40% between 2016-17 and 2018-19.

Time frame

  • • HMRC aims to make payment for claims or request further information within 28 days.
  • • The BFI (British Film Institute) aims to respond to applications for certificates within 28 days for VGTR
  • • BFI (DCMS) turnaround time is approximately 21 days for Film & TV tax relief
