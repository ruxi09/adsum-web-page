The Benefits Of VAT Bridging Loans And How They Work

  • By J T
  • Posted On September 1st, 2020

VAT bridging loans are a solution to accessing short-term funds. You can use this fund to pay due or unexpected VAT when buying commercial properties.

VAT bridging loans are a solution to accessing short-term funds. You can use this fund to pay due or unexpected VAT when buying commercial properties. It’s also needed when existing financing options aren’t enough to cover VAT. Unfortunately, many business owners are not aware of the additional requirements related to VAT, and, for many property buyers, VAT is often the final hurdle when closing the transaction. The rules of VAT can be quite complex and it’s essential to contact an experienced VAT accounting firm who can ensure that you have the necessary funding to meet any additional costs.

How VAT Bridging Loans Work

Property buyers can make VAT bridging loans work for them by contacting various lenders. The time needed to secure a VAT bridging loan may differ and most of the time, you can arrange it in less than a week. When the transaction is about to be concluded, VAT costs may escalate quickly and property buyers will appreciate any access to additional funding. VAT is typically not needed when purchasing residential properties, however, it is necessary when you purchase most commercial buildings. The VAT may increase the price of commercial properties by up to 20% in some cases and, because VAT negatively impacts the property market in the United Kingdom, it’s usually required only for newer properties that are less than 3 years old.

There are numerous benefits that property owners can get from VAT bridging loans:

· Quick Arrangement

There were numerous cases when buyers were caught unaware that they must pay VAT on recently purchased commercial properties. This often happens late in the transaction process. This means that buyers need to raise enough funds at short notice which can become challenging. Fortunately, buyers can quickly arrange a VAT bridging loan in less than a week with the aid of a VAT accounting firm.

· Straightforward Process

It’s easy and straightforward to apply for a VAT bridging loan. Most lenders are eager to see the completion of the deal and they will ensure a streamlined due diligence process.

· Free Up Essential Capital

Many property buyers have fixed budgets. Without VAT bridging loans, funds will be tied up and it won’t be possible to close the deal. A VAT bridging loan could ease cash flow and make it possible to close the deal sooner.

· Multiple Loan Applications

For real-estate investors, it essential for them to secure multiple bridging loans to pay VAT. It can be very time consuming and expensive if transactions can only be processed one at a time.
