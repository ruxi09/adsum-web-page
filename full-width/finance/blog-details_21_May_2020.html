How Does a VAT Bridging Loan Works?

  • By J T
  • Posted On May 21st, 202

With Adsum, the borrower is able to apply for a loan of £50,000+ with no upper limits, secured on the property purchased or other qualifying assets. Applications can be processed and funds delivered in as little as 48 hours, after which the full balance is repaid when HMRC repays the VAT. Bridging loans can be arranged over terms of anything from 60 days to 12 months, in accordance with the preferences of the borrower.

Adsum makes the money available speedily, the borrower uses it to pay the VAT due. When the VAT is refunded by HMRC, the funds are used to repay the loan in full, along with any additional borrowing costs incurred. The quicker the loan is repaid, the lower the overall borrowing costs. It’s as simple as that.

Adsum also finances the VAT on construction costs for property developments. We work with our clients to find good solutions to your funding needs.
